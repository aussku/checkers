# What Are Checkers
3 Player Checkers is a game based on a classic Checkers game where 2 players play agains each other. Checkers in itself is a strategy turn based board game, each player takes turns moving their checkers by specific rules, players must defend their checkers and defeat foe's checkers, game is considered finished once only one of the player's checkers remain on the board, the winner is the one who still has at least one of his checkers.

3 Player Checkers are mostly the same, the differences are in the board and movement rules are a bit different.

# Game Rules
- Each player has its own colour.
- Each player has 12 checkers.
- Each player starts in his own side of the board.
- Game is played in turns moving clockwise.
- There are 2 types of checkers: Regular and "King".
- If a checker reaches foe's back row, it turns into "King".

# Movement Rules
- Regular checkers
  - May move diagnoly to the front left or front right by one box, if the box is empty.
  - Cannot jump over or on teammates.
  - Foe's checker can be captured if the following conditions are met
    - Foe's checker is in either of these boxes from your checker: front left, front right, rear right, rear left.
    - There is an empty box straight after the foe's checker.
  - Multiple captures can be done during the same turn if the conditions above are true.
  - If your checker can capture foe's checker then your checker HAS to capture the checker, otherwise your checker will be taken off the board.

- King checkers
  - Is able to move in the folowing directions: front left, front right, rear right, rear left.
  - Is able to move more than one box, for example, if chosen direction is front left, checker may move 1, 2, 3 or however much boxes it desires.
  - May only move in a straight line unless capturing foe's checkers.
